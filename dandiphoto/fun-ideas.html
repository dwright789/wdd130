Fun Photography Ideas

#1- Get up close

Instead of getting a shot of the whole scene, zoom in or just simply move in closer. It's amazing how different things look when you really get up close. You can see so many details.

#2- Try a different angle

Sometimes straight on is perfect for what you want, but taking the picture from the side, from above, or down low can really make it better.

#3- Take some action shots

Pictures of people running or jumping can be fun! You may find yourself shoot a lot of these kinds of pictures hoping to get just one good shot out of the whole lot, but it's so rewarding when you finally get a great action picture.

#4- Silhouettes

In our “Simple Tips” we said to put the light on the front of the subject. For silhouettes, you want the light behind your subject. A sunset is a great time for this.

#5- Try something new

Take photos of something that isn’t your usual subject. If you normally take pictures of your kids, try photographing the tree out your window. If you normally focus on the flowers in your garden, try taking pictures of a big storm rolling in. This is a great way to learn new things by experimenting.
